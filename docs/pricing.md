## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is the free SDK really unlimited?

**Yes.** When self-hosted, there are no artificial limits. Your only limit is your hardware.

---

### What happens if I exceed my tier's limits?

**Options:**
1. Upgrade to the next tier
2. Purchase add-on capacity (when available)
3. Self-host (Pro features work offline)

**We'll notify you** before you hit limits.

---

### Can I try Pro before paying?

**Yes.** 30-day free trial when Pro launches (Q2 2025).

---

### Is there a discount for open-source projects?

**Yes.** Open-source projects get LingOS Pro for free.

**Apply when Pro launches** with:
- Link to your OSS project
- License verification (OSI-approved)
- Description of use case

---

### Is there an academic/education discount?

**Yes.** Students, teachers, and researchers get 50% off all paid tiers.

**Requirements:**
- Valid .edu email or equivalent
- Academic institution verification

---

### Can I pay annually?

**Yes.** Annual plans get 2 months free (effectively 17% discount).

---

### What payment methods are accepted?

**Planned:**
- Credit/debit cards (Stripe)
- PayPal
- Invoice (for Team/Enterprise)
- Cryptocurrency (select plans)

---

### Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

**Yes.** No long-term commitments.

**Upgrades:** Immediate
**Downgrades:** At end of billing period

---

### What happens if I cancel?

**You automatically downgrade to Free (Lite).**

**What you keep:**
- All your data (sessions, messages)
- Full SDK functionality
- LingOS Lite features

**What you lose:**
- Pro dialogue modes
- Advanced features
- Priority support

**No penalties.** Re-subscribe anytime.

---

### Is my data held hostage if I don't pay?

**No. Never.**

All your data is yours, stored locally (or in your chosen cloud). Canceling Pro doesn't affect your data or access to it.

**ActiveMirrorOS philosophy:** Your data belongs to you, period.

## Philosophy

### Why is the SDK free?

**We believe:**
- Memory should be a fundamental right for AI
- Open source accelerates innovation
- Privacy-first AI needs wide adoption
- Good tools should be accessible

**Our model:**
- SDK: Free forever (MIT license)
- Pro features: Pay for advanced capabilities
- Enterprise: Pay for support and scale

---

### What do you do with my payment data?

**We don't see it.** Payment processing via Stripe (PCI-compliant).

**We store:**
- Subscription tier
- Billing email
- Usage metrics (for billing)

**We don't store:**
- Credit card numbers
- Payment details
- Personal financial info
