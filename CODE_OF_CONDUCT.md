# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our ecosystem a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Focusing on what is best for the ecosystem.
- Showing empathy towards other community members.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported. The project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct.
